About Us

Just Enough is a language learning site anyone can use to learn just enough for their next vacation or trip. Created by five college students who all aspire to help others learn more about the world around them, just enough can help you do just that. By giving you flashcards with world applicable words and phrases to learn, you can then test your knowledge with our short quizzes, very soon you'll be speaking just like a native.
